About Me

I am Saif Bin Ilyas — a creator at the intersection of design, technology, and art. I am passionate about building immersive digital experiences that push the boundaries of what is possible on the web.

My work spans interactive 3D environments, visual storytelling, and web development. I believe that great design is not just about aesthetics — it is about crafting experiences that resonate, engage, and inspire.

About the 3D Gallery

The 3D Gallery integrated in this app is an interactive, browser-based virtual space that lets you walk through exhibition rooms in real time. Built with WebGL, it renders immersive environments directly in your browser — no plugins or downloads required.

Using your keyboard to move and your mouse to look around, you can explore curated art exhibitions as if you were physically present. The gallery supports multiple rooms, video artworks, and spatial audio — bringing the gallery experience to wherever you are.

This project represents my vision of merging cutting-edge web technology with art and culture — making creativity accessible to everyone, anywhere in the world.

Week 4
Students Burg Giebichenstein Kunsthochschule Halle
Binary Matter
During this summer term students of the Burg Giebichenstein University of Art and Design Halle developed positions on computational design and disseminated its impacts on aesthetics and society. Due to Corona restrictions the discourse was held mostly online and non-physical. Everybody gained insight into digital design strategies by delving into Grasshopper, a Rhino 3D tool for parametric design.

Within a short design sprint the students created a series of objects that have one thing in common: holes. When is a hole a hole? How holey can an object be? The results are exhibited in the Shutdown Gallery.
